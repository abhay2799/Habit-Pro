# Privacy Policy for Habit Pro

**Effective Date: June 15, 2026**

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at Habit Pro. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. 

When you use our mobile application (the "App"), we appreciate that you are trusting us with your personal habits and goals. We take your privacy very seriously. In this privacy policy, we seek to explain to you in the clearest way possible what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

## 1. Information We Collect
**We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.** 
Habit Pro is designed to be a completely offline, privacy-first application. 

### Local Storage
All of your data—including your habits, goals, tracking history, badges, and analytics—is stored entirely locally on your device's internal storage using secure local databases. We do not have servers, and we do not transmit your personal data across the internet.

### Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs. Your usage data remains strictly on your device.

## 2. Permissions We Request
To provide certain features, the App may request the following device permissions:
* **Notifications:** We request permission to send you local push notifications for your habit reminders and alarms. These are scheduled locally on your device and are not triggered by a remote server.
* **Alarms & Reminders:** We request permission to set exact alarms to ensure your habit reminders trigger reliably at your chosen times.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
Because we do not collect your information, we do not use it. Your data is yours alone.

## 4. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?
**No.** Since we do not collect or transmit your data off your device, it is impossible for us to share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties.

## 5. Security of Your Data
Your data is protected by the built-in security features of the Android operating system. We recommend that you keep a secure lock screen (PIN, pattern, or biometric) on your device to prevent unauthorized access to your locally stored Habit Pro data.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at our official developer email listed on the Google Play Store.
